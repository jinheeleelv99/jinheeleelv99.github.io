About This Work

This work proposes a deep learning-based skincare product recommendation system that analyzes both facial skin conditions and cosmetic ingredients. Unlike conventional methods that rely on customer reviews, our approach integrates AI-driven skin analysis and product efficacy prediction via ingredient analysis to provide personalized recommendations. By combining skin segmentation models and a transformer-based ingredient analyzer, this system ensures more reliable and science-backed skincare suggestions.

Skin Analysis

Our skin analysis models perform segmentation on six skin concerns: pores, redness, acne, wrinkles, pigmentation, and dark circles. Additionally, the segmentation model quantifies the results into numerical scores for better evaluation.

Recommendation Module

The Recommendation Engine matches a user's skin condition scores with the predicted ingredient effectiveness to rank and suggest the most suitable skincare products. By aligning individual skin concerns with optimal ingredient formulations, it ensures scientifically informed, data-driven recommendations rather than relying solely on user reviews or marketing claims.
